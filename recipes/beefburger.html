Beef Burger

Description

This is a recipe that explains how to make a Beef burger,very similar to chicken the first thing you need is to purchase an air fryer or you can do what I did and have your mom purchase one for you

The second thing you need is to buy Beefburgers you can get them from no frills much more expensive tho, a bag is eighteen dollars for like ten burgers. You then pop it in the air fryer for 20 minutes or in the oven if you need to.

Ingredients

  • Air Fryer
  • Beef Burgers
  • Lettuce
  • Burger Buns
  • Kraft cheese slices
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Pickles

    • Steps

  1. Open air fryer
  2. Put Burger in
  3. Set for 20 minutes
  4. Take burgers out
  5. Top with cheese and other ingredients
  6. Enjoy!
