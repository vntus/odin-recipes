Chicken Burger

Description

This is a recipe that explains how to make a chicken burger the first thing you need is to purchase an air fryer or you can do what I did and have your mom purchase one for you

The second thing you need is to buy chicken burgers you can get them from no frills if you're like me a bag is only ten dollars for like 16 burgers. You then pop it in the air fryer for 20 minutes or ten minutes if you need to.

Ingredients

  • Air Fryer
  • Chicken Burgers
  • Lettuce
  • Burger Buns
  • Kraft cheese slices

    • Steps

  1. Open air fryer
  2. Put Burger in
  3. Set for 20 minutes
  4. Take burgers out
  5. Top with cheese and other ingredients
  6. Enjoy!
